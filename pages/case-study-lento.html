The Brief

A restaurant demanding bespoke craftsmanship

Lento Restaurant required a comprehensive fit-out delivering custom furniture, bespoke signage, integrated lighting, and premium decorative elements for a contemporary dining destination in Um Al Sheif, Dubai. The scope covered complete supply and professional installation across every element of the interior.

The client's vision was a space defined by tactile materiality — raw steel and cast concrete balanced against warm leather upholstery, with a custom RAL colour palette unifying every painted surface. No off-the-shelf piece was specified; every item was designed and manufactured to the project's exact requirements.

The Challenge

Delivering a hospitality destination with no precedent

The brief required designing and manufacturing 18+ fully bespoke furniture pieces — from DJ tables with cast concrete tops to premium upholstered seating and a KAWS sculpture podium — alongside a complete four-sign signage system, integrated DJ lighting, glass block installations and stainless steel cladding, all within a single coordinated project scope.

Each element had to work as part of a unified material palette while being technically distinct: structural steel weldments, precision concrete casting, leather upholstery, architectural glazing, and surface cladding all required different fabrication disciplines and coordination on site.

Our Approach

Every piece resolved on drawing before production

Our team produced detailed fabrication drawings for every furniture piece, material panel, and signage element before a single cut was made. Steel and concrete structural elements were fabricated in-house at our workshop; leather upholstery was sourced to specification and fitted by specialist craftsmen. The RAL colour palette was finalised and signed off before any surface finishing began.

The DJ lighting system and glass block installations were coordinated within the main fit-out programme to eliminate re-work and ensure sequencing matched the construction timeline. The result: complete delivery — all 18+ furniture pieces, all four signs, all surface installations — to the client's exact specification.
