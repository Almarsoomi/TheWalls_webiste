Common Questions

Frequently asked

+
Do you offer a free consultation?
Yes, we offer a complimentary 30-minute consultation with our architects to discuss your requirements, space, timeline, and budget. This includes a site visit if needed.
+
How long does a typical fit-out take?
Timeline depends on scope: joinery-only projects take 2–4 weeks, complete villa interiors 8–16 weeks, office fit-outs 6–10 weeks. We provide a detailed project schedule after the design phase.
+
Do you work outside Dubai?
Yes, we work across the UAE (Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, etc.) and on request for GCC countries. Travel and logistics costs may apply depending on location and project scope.
+
Can you work from my existing design drawings?
Absolutely. We can build from any approved design drawings or work collaboratively with your existing interior designer. We're equally comfortable leading the design process from scratch.
+
What is the minimum project size?
There is no minimum. We undertake single custom pieces (bespoke wardrobes, TV units, decorative installations) as well as complete residential and commercial fit-outs.
+
Do you provide a warranty?
Yes, all custom joinery and installation work carries a 1-year workmanship warranty covering manufacturing defects and installation quality.
